Help & Support

General Information

Welcome to the Stock Market Platform. This platform allows you to track stock prices, view market trends, and make predictions based on stock data. If you're new, please follow the steps below to get started:

  • Navigate to the Dashboard to get an overview of your portfolio and market trends.
  • Use the Market Trends section to track live stock data and predictions.
  • Check the Archive to view historical stock data and performance trends.
  • Update your profile and account settings in the Settings section.
Account Management

Having trouble with your account? Here are some common solutions:

  • Forgot Password: Use the 'Forgot Password' link on the login page to reset your password. Check your email for reset instructions.
  • Updating Email Address: Go to the Settings page to update your email address.
  • Account Security: Your account security is important to us. Ensure that you use a strong, unique password, and never share your login details with anyone.
  • Deactivating Account: If you wish to deactivate your account, please contact our support team at support@stockplatform.com.
Using the Stock Market Platform

Follow these steps to make the most of the platform:

  • Viewing Stock Data: Use the Market Trends page to view real-time stock data and performance.
  • Predicting Stock Prices: In the Market Trends section, select a stock and view the predicted prices based on our machine learning models.
  • Archived Data: Go to the Archive section to view historical data and past performance of stocks.
  • Custom Reports: You can generate custom reports of stock performance by selecting the stocks and timeframes that matter to you.
